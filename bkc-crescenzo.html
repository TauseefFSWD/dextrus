Welcome toBKC, Crescenzo

Dextrus BKC Crescenzo is a premium coworking space located at Crescenzo Business District, G Block, Bandra Kurla Complex, Bandra East. The centre offers flexible desks, fixed desks, private offices, enterprise offices, meeting rooms and virtual office plans.

It serves startups, growing businesses, enterprises, freelancers, consultants and hybrid teams working out of Mumbai's central business district, with direct access to BKC's road network and nearby suburban rail connectivity.
