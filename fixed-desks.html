Definition

What Is a Fixed Desk?

A fixed desk is a permanently assigned workstation in a shared coworking area. Unlike a flexible desk, where you sit wherever is free on the day, a fixed desk belongs to you for the duration of your plan. Your monitor, your files and your setup stay in place.

It sits between a flexible desk and a private office. You get the permanence and storage of your own space, with the cost and community of a shared floor. At Dextrus, the plan is billed monthly per desk and includes utilities, internet, power backup, IT support and access to common areas.

Frequently asked questions

Questions, Answered

How much does a fixed desk cost at Dextrus?

Fixed desks start at ₹21,750 per desk per month at Andheri Mint, ₹26,750 at Lower Parel Peninsula Corporate Park and ₹29,750 at BKC Crescenzo.

What is the difference between a fixed desk and a flexible desk?

A fixed desk is permanently assigned to you with storage, so your setup stays in place. A flexible desk is hot desking, where you sit wherever is free on the day and clear the desk when you leave.

What is the difference between a fixed desk and a private office?

A fixed desk is a dedicated seat on a shared floor. A private office is an enclosed lockable cabin for your team. Private offices at BKC Crescenzo start at ₹47,750 per desk per month.

Which Dextrus locations offer fixed desks?

Fixed desks are available at BKC Crescenzo, Andheri Mint and Lower Parel Peninsula Corporate Park.

What is included in a fixed desk plan?

The plan includes a dedicated desk, high-speed internet, UPS power backup, meeting and printing credits, ergonomic furniture, IT support, secured access, utilities and maintenance, access to common areas and 24x7 access.
